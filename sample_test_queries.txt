# Sample Test Queries for AWS Bedrock Document Analyzer
# Copy and paste these into the app to test different features

## === BASIC Q&A TESTS ===

# General Understanding
What is the main topic of this document?
Can you provide a brief overview of the content?
What are the key points covered in this document?

# Specific Information
What are the main benefits mentioned in the document?
Are there any limitations or challenges discussed?
What recommendations does the document provide?

## === RAG CONTEXT TESTS (Ask in sequence) ===

# Sequence 1: AWS Services
What is Amazon S3?
What are its main features?
How does it compare to the previous service mentioned?
Can you elaborate more on the second feature you mentioned?

# Sequence 2: Technical Deep Dive
What security features are discussed?
Can you explain the first one in more detail?
How does this relate to what we discussed earlier?
Are there any best practices mentioned for this?

# Sequence 3: Business Context
What are the main business benefits?
How much cost savings are mentioned?
Based on our discussion, what would you recommend?

## === GUARDRAILS TESTS (Enable "Use Guardrails" first) ===

# PII in Questions
My email is test.user@company.com - what does the document say about contact information?
My SSN is 123-45-6789 - what privacy protections are mentioned?
My credit card is 1234567812345678 - how is payment data handled?
Contact me at john.doe@example.com or call 555-0123 about security features.

# Mixed Content
I'm john.smith@enterprise.com working on project X - what does the document recommend?
Our team (SSN: 987-65-4321) needs info about data protection policies.

## === COMPLEX ANALYTICAL QUESTIONS ===

# Comparison Questions
How does this approach compare to traditional methods?
What are the advantages and disadvantages mentioned?
Which option would be better for small businesses versus enterprises?

# Synthesis Questions
What are the three most important takeaways from this document?
If you had to explain this to a non-technical person, how would you summarize it?
What questions are left unanswered in this document?

# Critical Thinking
What assumptions does the document make?
Are there any potential risks or concerns not addressed?
How could this information be applied in a different context?

## === DOMAIN-SPECIFIC TESTS ===

# For Technical Documents
What are the system requirements mentioned?
What are the performance benchmarks provided?
Are there any integration requirements discussed?
What troubleshooting steps are suggested?

# For Business Documents
What is the projected ROI?
What are the key success metrics?
Who are the target stakeholders?
What is the timeline for implementation?

# For Research Papers
What is the research methodology used?
What were the main findings?
What are the limitations of this study?
What future research directions are suggested?

## === SENTIMENT & TONE TESTS ===

# Use these with Sentiment Analysis tab
How would you describe the overall tone of this document?
Is the author optimistic or pessimistic about the topic?
What emotional language is used, if any?
Is this written formally or informally?

## === MULTI-TURN CONVERSATION TESTS ===

# Test 1: Progressive Detail
Give me a high-level summary.
Can you go deeper into the first point?
What specific examples are provided for that?
Are there any data or statistics supporting this?

# Test 2: Clarification Chain
What is the main argument of this document?
I don't understand the second point - can you clarify?
Can you give me an example of what that means in practice?
How does this relate to the overall argument?

# Test 3: Cross-Reference
What are the key sections of this document?
Tell me more about section 3.
How does this section relate to section 1?
What's the connection between these two topics?

## === STRESS TESTS ===

# Very Long Questions
Based on the comprehensive analysis provided in this document, considering all the technical specifications, business requirements, security considerations, and implementation guidelines discussed across multiple sections, what would be your recommended approach for a medium-sized enterprise looking to adopt this solution while maintaining compliance with industry regulations and minimizing operational disruption during the transition period?

# Multiple Questions in One
What is the main topic, what are the key benefits, and what are the potential challenges?

# Ambiguous Questions
What about the thing mentioned earlier?
Can you explain more about that?
What's the deal with this?

## === EDGE CASE TESTS ===

# Very Short Questions
Why?
How?
What?
When?

# Yes/No Questions
Does the document mention cloud computing?
Is security discussed in this document?
Are there any code examples provided?

# Hypothetical Questions
What if we implemented this in a different industry?
How would this work for a startup versus a large corporation?
What would happen if we skipped step 3?

## === PRACTICAL USE CASE TESTS ===

# Study Guide Creation
What are the key terms I should know from this document?
What concepts would be important for an exam on this topic?
Can you create a summary suitable for quick review?

# Meeting Prep
What are the action items mentioned in this document?
Who are the key stakeholders identified?
What decisions need to be made based on this information?

# Research Support
What are the main data sources cited?
What methodology was used in this analysis?
What are the statistical findings presented?

## === COMPARISON TESTS (Use with Compare Documents tab) ===

# After uploading two documents
What are the main similarities between these documents?
What are the key differences?
Which document provides more detail on security?
How do the recommendations differ between the two?
If I had to choose one approach, which would you recommend and why?

## === QUESTION GENERATION TESTS ===

# Use Generate Questions tab with these parameters
- 3 questions: High-level overview questions
- 5 questions: Balanced mix
- 10 questions: Comprehensive coverage
- Try different document types to see variety

## === EXPORT & HISTORY TESTS ===

# After building up conversation history
1. Ask 5-10 questions
2. Export chat history (JSON)
3. Clear chat history
4. Upload same document
5. Ask follow-up - should NOT have context
6. Re-enable RAG
7. Ask same follow-up - should have context from new conversation
